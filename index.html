The Operator Behind Olaf

From Marine Corps discipline to mortgage and capital strategy.

For more than 25 years I've worked in mortgage lending and executive leadership in Hawaii — delivering operational scale and high-touch structuring for the nuanced borrower scenarios unique to the islands.

Since founding my advisory firm in 2020, the focus has widened beyond traditional lending. I advise non-QM and wholesale mortgage companies on national growth, sales structure, recruiting, and scalable operations; originate residential loans through a licensed relationship with HomeMac Mortgage; and structure select hard money and commercial transactions. I also partner with investors in distressed real estate — capital alignment, stabilization, repositioning, and disposition — and helped establish a private investment vehicle focused on distressed real estate and specialty finance.

Across every venture the objective is the same: align capital, strategy, and execution to produce durable results. If you're looking for a strategic mortgage advisor, a capital partner, or a thoughtful operator focused on disciplined growth, I welcome the conversation.
