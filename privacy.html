Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Note: This policy is a working draft intended for review by counsel before launch. It is provided for transparency and is not legal advice.

Olaf LLC ("Olaf," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit olafllc.com or contact us, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Information you provide: when you submit our contact form, book a call, or email or call us, we collect details such as your name, email address, phone number, and the information in your message.
  • Automatically collected information: like most websites, we may collect standard technical data (such as IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and referring source) through cookies or analytics tools to understand site usage.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide the services you request (advisory, lending, or fund).
  • To communicate with you about your request and our services.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations, including those applicable to mortgage activity.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate (for example, scheduling, email, and analytics tools), under appropriate confidentiality obligations.
  • Lending partners, including Home Mortgage Alliance Corporation (HMAC), where you request mortgage-related services and to the extent needed to process your request.
  • Authorities or others where required by law, or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

Cookies & analytics

We may use cookies and third-party analytics to understand how visitors use the site. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling them may affect site functionality.

Your choices & rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, or opt out of marketing communications, by contacting us. Depending on your state of residence (including under the California Consumer Privacy Act), you may have additional rights regarding your personal information.

Data security & retention

We use reasonable measures to protect the information we collect and retain it only as long as needed for the purposes described above or as required by law.

Children's privacy

Our website is intended for business audiences and is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
